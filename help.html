How to watch a video

Click on a thumbnail or the title of a video you wish to watch on the frontpage. You can also download the video depending on the video player you are using. This depends on your browser.

Download a video

Follow the instructions below the video to download it in full quality. Make sure you have 7Zip or WinRAR ready as videos greater than 25 MB will be in multiple files.
